My Story

A journey through code, craft, and curiosity.

I am a Computer Science student, builder, and technical generalist based in Taxila, Pakistan. My route into computing was not a straight line—it was shaped by an early curiosity about natural systems, a gap year focused on tactile craft, and a deliberate pivot from Pre-Medical studies to software engineering.

I value code written from first principles, clean architecture, and the synthesis of different domains. Whether building a modular MVC structure in PHP or writing systems software in C++, I aim to work with clarity and discipline.

The Timeline

2006 – 2021

Early Life & Roots

Born in Lahore, I spent my early years there before moving to Taxila, a historic valley known for its ancient Buddhist civilization and traditional stone carving. Growing up here fostered my early interest in history and quiet observations.

2021 – 2023

FSc Pre-Medical Pathway

Pursued Pre-Medical studies during high school, driven by a deep fascination with biological systems and organic details. Although my professional direction eventually shifted, this background instilled in me a rigorous study discipline and appreciation for complex systems.

2023 – 2024

The Gap Year: Polymath Exploration

A self-directed year that proved to be a major turning point. I dedicated myself to hands-on crafts—learning crochet, working with clay, origami, and wood carving. During these months, I also taught myself programming fundamentals with Python and began exploring languages, learning that focus is a muscle and building is a unified act.

2024 – Present

Computer Science at UET Taxila

Pivoted to Computer Science, enrolling at UET Taxila. Finding that programming is the ultimate medium for creation, I’ve focused on learning the core concepts of software engineering from the ground up: OOP paradigms, file persistence, and backend data structures without relying on heavy frameworks.

Beyond the Terminal

Reading & Philosophy

I read widely across history, economics, and Islamic intellectual thought. I am fascinated by systems of order, human behavior, and the philosophical weight of Allama Iqbal's poetry.

Macro Photography

Focusing on the quiet details of nature. My macro work captures the intricate engineering of insects and plants, training my eye for precise alignment and structural clarity.

Chess & Strategy

A hobby that exercises analytical depth. The precise calculations, spatial patterns, and positional strategy of chess directly translate into my code structuring.

Table Tennis

A sport that keeps reflexes sharp and coordinates quick decision-making under speed, balancing out long hours of sitting in front of a screen.
